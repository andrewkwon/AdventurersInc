Every quest has a set of skills associated with it
An adventurer with a skill set that matches the associated skills well will likely do well on the quest

Skills:
Combat - ability in fights
Magic - knowledge of arcane arts
Science - knowledge of the natural world
Survival - ability to endure harsh environments
Communication - for interacting with others
Stealth - ability to act unnoticed
Research - information gathering and analysis
Arts - artistic and creative ability
Strategy - planning
Improvisation - ability to think on their feet
Healing - ability to fix minor injuries
Perseverence - ability to handle longer or more intense missions
